Submissions, a Play in 4 Acts.

1. Connect with Orange Pekoe Press

Use your Google or Facebook account to connect with Orange Pekoe Press. Connecting will take you to your dashboard, the center of your interactions with Orange Pekoe Press.

2. Submit Your Story

Enter basic information about your story—title, author's name, text, whether or not it is a simultaneous submission, that sort of thing. You can find answers to questions about the submission process in the FAQ.

3. Preview Story Appearance

Immediately after submitting, you'll be shown a webpage demonstrating how Orange Pekoe Press's computers see your submission. Check this over for problems, and flag it for formatting errors if you see them, then submit a new version. You can also see this at any time in your dashboard.

4. Watch for Updates

We will email you when your story is accepted or rejected, but you can also check your story's status on your dashboard.
