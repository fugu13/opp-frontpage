Welcome to

Orange Pekoe Press,

a new short fiction ebook publisher, now accepting submissions of up to around twelve thousand words in

  • speculative fiction,
  • literary fiction,
  • dramatic fiction,
  • and translated fiction

via this website. The first round of submissions may take a while to review, though we don't intend for any submission to take longer than twelve weeks. The first round of acceptances should go out sooner than that; twelve weeks is just the outside amount of time to sort through everything. Especially because of those possible delays, simultaneous submissions are fine so long as you inform us of the situation.

Short fiction is ready to retake lost ground in the world of publishing with the increasing popularity of the ebook. You can read more about Orange Pekoe Press as the process goes on at the Orange Pekoe Press blog (recent entries shown below).

Stories will be made available on all major ebook platforms for one dollar a story.
